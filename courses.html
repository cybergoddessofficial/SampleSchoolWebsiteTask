Courses Offered

Departments

We offers undergraduate programmes, which lead to the award of Bachelors in Arts, Commerce, B usiness Administration and Science degrees from Shanimz University. Each UG programme is led by a Programme Coordinator. Members of the teaching faculty who teach these programmes are organized into five broad departments, each led by a Head of the Department. These Departments are:

  • Business Management
  • Commerce
  • English and Languages
  • Sciences
  • Social Sciences and Communication

Undergraduate Programmes

The undergraduate programmes offered are:

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Arts (BA)– Mass Communication & Journalism, Political Science, English ML
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) – Advertising, Sales Promotion and Sales Management
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) – Business Analytics
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) – Computer Applications
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) – General
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) – Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) – Mathematics, Electronics, Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) – Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Chemistry
